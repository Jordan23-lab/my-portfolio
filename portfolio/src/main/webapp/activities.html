Soccer

I used to dislike the sport and mainly played because my dad forced me to, but after a few years I started to get good at the sport I started to enjoy it. I continued playing throughout high school eventually becoming captain of my varsity soccer team and getting recruited to play for some college teams. I chose not to play in college, but I do play in the soccer club at Stony Brook.
